The soul of Tea Time

The passion and pride of working in the world of tea have been the engines of the evolution of Tea Time since its birth and to this day. Our origins go back to a dream: create an exquisite tea brand, capable of becoming a reference. It all started in 2018 when, 3 friends passionate of tea embarked on an adventure that seemed impossible at the time: to create a high-quality tea website. This is how Tea Time was born, a brand specializing in fresh, bulk tea and all the accessories needed to enjoy each and every cup of tea!

Our team

The three of us met at the Code First: Girls course in Leeds and bonded over our shared passion for coding and tea. Our diverse origins, and the fact that we are all tea lovers, inspired us to create a place to share our passion of enjoying the moment of drinking tea and share it with the whole world! We are hoping to show the different teas we can find in the world and build a tea-lovers community who love tea as much as we do.
